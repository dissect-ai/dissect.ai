DissectAI

Real-Time AI Detection of Aortic Dissection from Chest CT Scans

The Problem

Aortic dissection is a rare but deadly condition that often goes undiagnosed during emergency visits. Quick and accurate detection can save lives, but current methods rely heavily on expert radiologists and can be slow or inconsistent.

The Solution

DissectAI is an AI-powered tool that analyzes chest CT scans within 60 seconds to detect signs of aortic dissection and classify its type, assisting emergency physicians and radiologists to make faster, life-saving decisions.

How It Works

Using advanced 3D deep learning models and natural language explanation generated by AI, DissectAI highlights critical features in CT images and provides a clear, actionable report.
